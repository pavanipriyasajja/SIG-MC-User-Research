# Kubernetes Multi-Cluster UX Research

## What is multi cluster user research?

Deploying, managing and monitoring multiple Kubernetes clusters is <b>challenging</b>. Our user research <b>aims</b> to capture engineers’ experiences and expectations with multi-cluster setups and responsible for multi cluster operations.

Our <b>goal</b> is to make complex multi cluster systems easy to use and turn difficult multi cluster operations into simple human friendly. For more information please read this <a href="https://docs.google.com/document/d/1uCyHpI5zV1ME9qBtGjLkrIhpl3iXe-zQsn0SSbBiAE4/edit?usp=sharing">proposal doc.</a> 

## Our research will help

<li>Identifies real <b>pain points</b> in multi-cluster operations</li>

<li>Understands engineers’ <b>workflows, needs, and expectations</b></li>

<li>Informs better, <b>user-centered solution</b> design</li>

<li>Reduces complexity and <b>improves usability</b></li>

<li>Makes multi-cluster operations more <b>efficient and easier</b> to manage</li>

## So far what we did: 

We conducted the surveys one of the user research method and produced the results in the KubeCon NA 2025 (<a href="https://sched.co/27Nlj">Presentation and video</a> )

## 📌 Status of the Project:

Currently we are working on transforming survey results into Hybrid engineer persona and journey mapping and we are presenting them in the KubeCon EU 2026, <a href="https://sched.co/2EF5b">Talk information</a> 

## Our next steps:

We are conducting in-depth User interviews to gather more information about multi cluster challenges and expectations from the engineers to understand tools, workflows, and daily practices<a href="https://multicluster.sigs.k8s.io/">Learn more from our website</a>
